About me

Hey I'm Roman, nice to meet you.
Ever since I booted my first Windows 3.1 machine, I was intrigued by technology.

Having dreamed about creating my own programs, years went by as 'coding' was something that was out of my league. But dreams never fade.

My day job was not facing me with any more challenges, complexity or obstacles. I felt lost, I had to step it up.

I changed my mindset from is coding something I can do? To coding is something I can do. I have never looked back.

Every little project I developed, I proudly showed off to my relatives. I was in love.

Continuously seeking for that edge in my current skillset, my apps went from simple, poorly laid out projects, to responsive highly interactive applications.

I aspire to become a professional developer, that works alongside other bright minds on applications that impact the world.
